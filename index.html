Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Thank you for using Shopify Profit Calculator ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use our browser extension.

Summary: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All calculation data and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not track you.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Our extension is designed to work entirely on your device, without sending any data to our servers.

The extension does NOT:

  • Collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • Require you to create an account or log in
  • Track your browsing activity or websites you visit
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Send any data to third parties

2. Data Storage

All calculation data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using browser storage (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your device.

Data stored locally on your device includes:

  • Your calculation preferences (default country, currency, theme settings)
  • Calculation history records
  • License activation status (if you purchase a premium plan)

We have no access to this data and cannot view, modify, or delete it. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser storage.

3. Permissions We Use

Our extension requires minimal permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your calculation preferences and history locally on your device

We do not request access to your browsing data, tabs, or any other sensitive permissions.

4. Payment Processing

If you choose to purchase a premium license, payments are processed through Payhip, our trusted third-party payment provider.

When you make a purchase:

  • Your payment information is handled directly by Payhip
  • We do not receive or store your credit card details
  • Your purchase is subject to Payhip's Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data is as secure as your browser and device. We recommend keeping your browser and operating system up to date to ensure security.

Because we do not collect or store your data on our servers, there is no risk of your data being breached or accessed by us.

6. Children's Privacy

Our extension is intended for general use and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any of your personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete.

You can:

  • Clear all local data by uninstalling the extension
  • Contact us with any questions about your privacy

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
