Privacy Policy for Ecom Profit Calculator

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Thank you for using Ecom Profit Calculator ("we", "our", or "us"). This is a Microsoft Edge browser extension designed exclusively for online store owners, dropshippers and ecommerce entrepreneurs to calculate net profit, platform transaction fees, product margins and pricing strategies for their online selling listings. Your privacy is our top priority, and this document fully explains our data handling rules.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

Critically Important: This extension NEVER collects, uploads, transmits or stores your store API keys, admin login credentials, store order data, customer personal information, platform billing details or any sensitive store backend data to external servers.

We also do not track your browsing history, collect IP addresses, or gather any personally identifiable user information for advertising or analytics purposes.

2. Local Browser Storage Usage (Matching Edge Extension storage Permission)

We only request the browser `storage` permission to save non-sensitive, user-customized preferences and calculation history locally on your own device only. No data leaves your browser. Saved local data includes:

  • Default country, currency and platform fee preset values
  • Extension dark/light theme display settings
  • Historical calculation records: product cost, selling price, shipping cost, payment processing fees, profit margin, ROI, break-even results
  • Reverse pricing calculation history for online product listing optimization

This local storage only serves to let you review past product profit calculations and retain personalized settings, with zero third-party sharing or server upload.

3. No Remote Code & No Third-Party Platform API Access

This extension does not embed remote executable code, third-party tracking scripts, or connect to any e-commerce official APIs. All profit calculation logic runs fully offline within your Edge browser. It only lets you manually input your online store’s fee & cost figures for manual computation, with no automatic data sync to any external selling platform backend.

4. License & Payment Terms (Aligned with Edge Store Listing)

Upgrade licenses are processed via independent third-party processor Payhip. We cannot access or store your credit card, PayPal or checkout billing data; all payment security is managed by Payhip under their own privacy policy.

Our one-time purchase licensing tiers for online sellers:
• Free Tier: Basic profit & margin calculator (permanent free access)
• Basic License ($9.99 one-time lifetime): Unlock advanced calculation features
• Pro License ($19.99 one-time lifetime): Full access including batch product calculation, break-even analysis and reverse pricing tools
All plans are one-off purchases with no recurring subscriptions or auto-renewals.

5. Third-Party Services

The only integrated third-party service is Payhip for license checkout. No other analytics, ad tracking, or data-sharing partners are integrated into this Edge extension.

6. Children’s Privacy

This tool is built exclusively for adult ecommerce merchants and business operators aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors under 13 years of age, and will remove relevant local data traces upon user request.

7. Your Data Rights

You may clear all locally saved extension data at any time via your Edge browser’s extension management settings or by clearing browser local storage data. Since no user data is sent to our remote servers, there is no centralized database of your information to submit deletion requests for.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this privacy policy occasionally to comply with regulatory requirements or product function updates. All revisions will be published to this GitHub-hosted page with an updated "last updated" timestamp.

9. Contact Information

For any privacy-related inquiries or concerns, please reach out via the developer contact email submitted within your Edge extension developer dashboard.
